# SETU TRACK — competitive assessment

## Honest position
SETU TRACK is not yet at Deel / Remote / Rippling maturity.

## Where SETU TRACK can realistically win sooner
Instead of trying to beat global compliance platforms head-on, SETU TRACK should aim to win in these areas first:
- contractor workflow clarity
- project-linked payroll visibility
- manager and admin actionability
- export and reporting readiness for agencies and service teams
- calmer, more premium daily UX

## What category leaders do better today
- broader compliance coverage
- richer integrations
- deeper analytics and audit tooling
- more polished end-to-end reliability
- more consistent product language across every screen

## What SETU TRACK should become
A financial and operational command center for contractor teams.

## Product priorities
### 1. Command center dashboard
Answer instantly:
- what needs attention
- what is blocked
- what payroll will cost
- which projects are at risk
- what was recently approved, closed, exported, or paid

### 2. Better reporting
Make payroll and exports more useful with:
- trend cards
- variance
- project budget risk
- cleaner register views
- stronger receipt and paid-state traceability

### 3. Better execution quality
- fewer page-local data assumptions
- stronger error states
- consistent loading and empty states
- route-by-route QA discipline
